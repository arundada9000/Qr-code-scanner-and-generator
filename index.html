Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This tool operates primarily on the client-side:

  • Local Processing: All QR generation and scanning happen in your browser. We do not store your data on our servers.
  • Camera Access: Camera permissions are used strictly for scanning and are not recorded.
  • Cookies: We use local storage only to save your scan history for your convenience.
